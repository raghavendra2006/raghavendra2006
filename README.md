## 💡 DevOps Philosophy

> *"Automate everything repeatable. Monitor everything critical. Document everything important."*

- **Shift Left** — Integrate security and testing early in the pipeline
- **GitOps First** — Infrastructure and configs live in Git, always
- **Observability > Monitoring** — Metrics, logs, and traces together tell the full story
- **Fail Fast, Recover Faster** — Design for resilience, not just uptime
